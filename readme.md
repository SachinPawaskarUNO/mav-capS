# *Microfinancing made simple*
CapSphere Services is a Malaysian-based peer-to-peer (P2P) platform, connecting small and medium-sized enterprises
(SMEs) to private investors. The company enables SMEs to raise capital efficiently at lower rates while offering lenders a higher return on investment. CapSphereâ€™s platform will utilize a simple and effective financing process to improve customer experience and reduce the
